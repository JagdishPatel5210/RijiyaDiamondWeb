About

RIJIYA DIAMONDS

Rijiya diamonds being one of the respected Manufacturer of polished diamonds Is still consistently improving and enhancing the art of diamond procuring, sorting, cleaving, cutting, and polishing with deep passion and integrity.

Our values

Integrity, transparency, accountability and trust are our key values that we maintain and fulfill in all aspects of dealing with our employees and clients.
